About Mushroom Finder

Thank you for clicking on the About page, If you are trying to learn more about this website and how it came to be, then you have come to the right place! This Mushroom Finder website was originally designed to be hosted in an application for iPhone and Android smart phones. We are still committed into making this possible so that mushroom foragers can download the app easily and use it while on their way to unique mushrooms discovery. For now, this website contains all of the information necessary for helping you locate your mushrooms of interest near you.
