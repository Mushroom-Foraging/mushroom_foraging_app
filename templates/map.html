Mushrooms are scientifically known as fungi, and they have been growing along side humans since the beginning of time. There are many edible mushrooms around us, from the ones you find in the store to the ones you find in the woods. However, foraging mushrooms in the woods is not an easy task and requires a lot of knowledge and experience.

This is a mushroom finder app, any mushrooms you find can be a poisonous look alike. Please consult with a professional mycologist before consuming any mushrooms you are unsure of.
